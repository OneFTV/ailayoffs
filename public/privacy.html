Privacy Policy

Effective: January 1, 2026 · Last updated: February 2026 · Version 1.0

AILayoff.live is a public data tracker. We do not sell personal data. We do not require account creation to access any of our information. This policy explains what limited data we collect and why.

01 Who We Are

AILayoff.live is a free, publicly accessible platform that aggregates and visualizes data on AI-driven job displacement worldwide. Our mission is to provide transparent, real-time information about the impact of artificial intelligence on global employment, sourced from public reports by organizations such as layoffs.fyi, Goldman Sachs, McKinsey, and the World Economic Forum.

We are not affiliated with any of the companies listed in our tracker, nor with the organizations whose research we cite.

02 Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate and improve the platform:

  • Usage Analytics: Page views, session duration, geographic region, device type, and referral source — collected in aggregate, without identifying individuals.
  • Newsletter Subscriptions: If you voluntarily subscribe to our weekly report, we collect your email address. Nothing else.
  • Contact Submissions: If you contact us via email or a form, we retain your message and email address solely to respond.
  • Server Logs: Standard web server logs (IP address, request timestamp, HTTP response codes) are retained for up to 30 days for security and performance monitoring.

When you submit a contact form, we also collect your name and email address to respond to your inquiry.

03 How We Use Your Information

  • To send you the newsletter you requested (email subscribers only)
  • To understand how users interact with our tracker so we can improve it
  • To respond to support or data correction requests
  • To monitor for abuse or unusual traffic patterns

We use your data only for the purposes described above and to support our data products and services.

04 Data Sharing

We may share or make available data in the following circumstances:

  • Infrastructure providers: Railway (cloud hosting), Cloudflare (CDN and DDoS protection). These providers process data solely to operate the service.
  • Analytics and insights: We may share aggregate, anonymized usage data with partners or clients.
  • Data licensing: The tracker data we compile (company names, displacement figures, sector classifications, and related public information) may be licensed to third parties for research, media, or commercial purposes. See our Data Licensing page for details.
  • Legal requirements: If required by applicable law, court order, or regulatory authority.

06 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR (EU), LGPD (Brazil), CCPA (California), or other applicable privacy laws, including:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to withdraw consent (e.g., unsubscribing from the newsletter)
  • The right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

07 About Our Data

The displacement figures and company tracker on AILayoff.live are compiled from publicly available reports and filings. We do not collect personal data about workers who have been laid off. All individuals referenced in our tracker are identified only in their professional capacity (company name, job function category, volume of roles affected) as reported in public sources.

If you believe any data point on our platform is inaccurate or should be updated, please contact us — we are committed to data accuracy as a core editorial standard.
