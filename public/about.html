About AILayoff.live

Our Mission

AILayoff.live exists to provide transparent, real-time tracking of AI-driven job displacement worldwide. We believe the workforce impact of artificial intelligence deserves the same rigorous, data-first tracking that COVID-19 received from Worldometer.

Independence

We are fully independent and not affiliated with any technology company, staffing agency, or political organization. Our data speaks for itself — we don't advocate for or against AI adoption. We simply track what's happening.

Our Approach

  • Data-first: Every number is sourced and classified by confidence level
  • Transparent: Methodology and sources are fully documented
  • Real-time: Updated daily with breaking events added within hours
  • Global: Tracking displacement across 48+ countries

Inspiration

Inspired by trackers like Worldometer and layoffs.fyi, we saw the need for a dedicated platform focused specifically on AI's impact on employment — not just tech layoffs in general, but the specific displacement caused by AI and automation technologies.
