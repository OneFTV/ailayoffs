About AILayoff.live

AILayoff.live is an independent, real-time tracker of AI-driven job displacement. Launched in 2025, it provides transparent, sourced data on how artificial intelligence is impacting employment globally. Our methodology is inspired by Worldometer's approach to real-time data tracking.
