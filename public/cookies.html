Effective: January 1, 2026 · Last updated: February 2026

Our Approach

AILayoff.live is designed to be privacy-friendly. We minimize cookie usage and do not use cookies for advertising or tracking across websites.

What We Don't Use

  • No advertising cookies
  • No third-party tracking cookies
  • No social media tracking pixels
  • No Google Analytics or similar third-party analytics

Cloudflare Cookies

We use Cloudflare for security and performance. Cloudflare may set essential cookies to protect against bots and DDoS attacks. These cookies are necessary for the site to function and cannot be disabled.

Local Storage

We use browser localStorage (not cookies) to store an anonymous visitor identifier for our self-hosted analytics. This data never leaves your browser except as an anonymous aggregate count. You can clear this at any time via your browser settings.

Your Choices

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Essential Cloudflare cookies are required for the site to function. Our analytics use localStorage, which you can clear under your browser's site data settings.
